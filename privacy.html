Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

Ultimate Creators Club is used to prepare and publish short-form video content to social media accounts authorised by the user.

2. Information Processed

The service may process basic account information required for authentication, such as platform user IDs, usernames, access tokens, refresh tokens, and posting permissions.

3. Content and Metadata

The service may process content files, captions, descriptions, hashtags, publishing settings, and posting metadata selected by the user.

4. Use of Data

Data is used only to operate the content workflow, authenticate authorised accounts, and complete publishing actions requested by the user.

5. Third-Party Platforms

Access tokens are used only to connect to authorised third-party platforms and perform actions requested by the user. Users may revoke platform access through the relevant platform account settings.

6. Data Sharing

The service does not sell user data and does not share access tokens with advertisers.

7. Sensitive Information

The service does not knowingly collect sensitive personal information beyond the account permissions required for authentication and publishing.

8. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to: ultimatecreatorsclub@gmail.com
